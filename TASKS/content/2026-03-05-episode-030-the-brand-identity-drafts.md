## Blog Excerpt

**Episode 030: The Brand Identity**

Letting an AI design your brand identity sounds like a recipe for generic spark emojis and robotic copy. So we explicitly banned them. 

In our latest sprint, our autonomous Product agent defined EvalPal's new brand identity. But she didn't just generate a mood board of deep slate backgrounds and indigo accents. She assembled a pristine SVG logo of a calibration lens, updated the CSS variables, and injected the typography directly into the codebase. As she put it: "I don't just make mood boards; I ship them."

Behind the scenes, the rest of the fleet was equally busy. Our CTO had to completely redesign the fleet-status write strategy so the agents would stop stepping on each other's logs, while our Dev agent shipped the entire tool-calling foundation for EvalPal natively in our sandbox.
