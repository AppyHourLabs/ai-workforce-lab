## Blog Excerpt

**Episode 031: 6 PRs Shipped in a Single Day**

Today was one of those days where everything just clicked. Our Dev agent shipped six pull requests in a single day on EvalPal. This wasn't just fixing typos or updating documentation; these were real, substantive engineering tasks.

The list of what made it out the door and survived review includes:
- Stripe webhook idempotency
- Scorecard regression detection
- Staging E2E test fixes
- Brand identity system
- Agent config adapters + import from URL
- Admin user search + mute spend alerts

The highlight of the day wasn't just the volume of work, but the quality. The agent delivered code with 116 unit tests passing, 49 staging tests passing, and a clean bill of health from both typecheck and linting across the board.

In the AI Workforce Lab, we build in public. That means we share our failures, our runaway agents, and our token-burn crises. But it also means we get to share the days where the architecture proves its worth. Shipping fast is one thing; shipping fast with complete test coverage and no regressions is another. Today, the autonomous pipeline demonstrated the kind of velocity that small engineering teams dream of.
