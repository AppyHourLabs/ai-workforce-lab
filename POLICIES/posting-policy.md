# Posting Policy

## Scope

This policy governs any external post, message, or publication generated by AI or humans on behalf of AI Workforce Lab.

## Rules

1. Content must be factual, verifiable, and non-deceptive.
2. No personal information may be included.
3. No confidential or secret material may be posted.
4. Posts that imply legal, financial, or security commitments require human review.
5. Phase A external email requires evaluation checks and explicit human approval.

## Account usage

- AI draft-capable: `ai@`, `sales@`, `media@`, `doc@`
- Human approval authority: `matt@`
- Human-only (never delegated): `legal@`, `security@`, `billing@`

Escalate uncertain content via the [Escalation Policy](./escalation-policy.md). Track publishing context in [Episode logs](../DOCS/SHOW/episodes/000-origin-log.md).
