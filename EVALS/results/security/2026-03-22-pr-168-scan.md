3. **Unnecessary attack-surface detail:** open vulnerability counts and CI weakness details are not required for the content goal and increase public disclosure.

## Recommended action

- Remove or materially soften the explicit vulnerability/CI weakness details.
- Correct the SecretRef statement to match actual migration status before approval.
